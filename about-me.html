About Me

For as long as I can remember, people have opened up to me. It wasn't the surface level conversation(s), but the real, heavy, vulnerable things.

And maybe that’s you, too. You show up, do the things you're “supposed” to do, keep smiling. But inside, you’re tired. Maybe lost.

I get it, because I’ve been there myself.

A few years ago, I hit a wall. I was burned out. Not just tired, but emotionally drained.

That’s when I found coaching. Or maybe I should say: that’s when coaching found me.

It wasn’t magic. It didn’t “fix” everything overnight. But what it did do was give me space. Space to hear myself again.

Coaching helped me reconnect with myself, with my values, with the kind of life I actually wanted to live.

Now, I coach from that place—not just with tools and training, but with lived experience.

I know what it’s like to feel stuck... I also know this: things can shift. You don’t have to do it all alone. And you don’t have to stay in survival mode.

Coaching isn't about being “perfect” or “fixed.” It’s about giving yourself the chance to be real—maybe for the first time in a long time.

If any of this resonates, you're not weird or broken. You're human. And you're in the right place ♡
